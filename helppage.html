Welcome to the Hoobfarba Help Page, how may I help you.

Movement: North, north, N, n, or any of the equivalents for the other cardinal directions. You can also climb up and down, or simply go up or go down, depending on where you are.

Actions: The various actions that the user can perform include taking objects, cleaning, paying, eating, dancing, getting funky, taking a leak, playing with your dog, and grabbing things.

You can also request a smaller help menu if you input help and hit do action. The same goes for requesting the score.
